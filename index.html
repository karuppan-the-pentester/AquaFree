Automated Planting and Seeding

Capability to automatically plant seeds in predefined patterns or according to user-defined specifications using a gripper.



Precision Watering System

Automated and precise control over the watering of plants, considering factors like soil moisture levels, plant types, and growth stages.



Sensors Integration

Integration of various sensors (pH, temperature, air quality, humidity, soil moisture, light intensity, UV) to collect real-time data for environmental monitoring.
